## What Has Been Delivered

The following modules are fully functional — both backend and frontend — and ready for your staff to use from day one:

### 1. Authentication & Access Control
- Secure login for all staff roles and patients
- Role-based access: each user sees only what their role permits
- Patient self-registration portal
- Staff invite system: admin sends a one-time secure link; staff set their own password; links expire in 48 hours

### 2. Patient Management
- Patient registration (self-registration or receptionist-assisted)
- Patient search by name, phone, or email
- Full patient profiles with demographics, appointments, and medical records

### 3. Appointments
- Booking, rescheduling, and cancellation
- Full appointment history per patient
- Today's schedule view for the receptionist

### 4. Medical Records
- Consultation notes, diagnosis, and treatment plans
- Linked to the appointment at which they were created
- Full record history accessible to authorised staff and the patient themselves

### 5. Laboratory Module
- Doctors order lab tests directly from a medical record
- Lab technicians manage the full order lifecycle: Ordered → Sample Collected → Processing → Completed
- Lab technicians enter results; doctors review and mark results as reviewed or requiring follow-up
- Results visible to the ordering doctor

### 6. Pharmacy & Drug Inventory
- Doctors issue prescriptions from within a medical record
- Prescription queue for the pharmacist
- Full dispense recording with quantity
- Drug inventory catalogue with stock tracking
- Low-stock alert banner when any drug falls below threshold
- Automatic stock deduction on dispense

### 7. Staff Management
- Admin creates and manages all staff accounts
- Instant account deactivation if a staff member leaves
- Resend invite for staff who missed their link

### 8. Inpatient Module
- Ward and bed management (admin creates wards and assigns beds)
- Real-time bed occupancy overview
- Patient admission linked to a bed and medical record
- Nursing and doctor monitoring notes during admission
- Full admission lifecycle: Admitted → Transferred → Discharged
- Discharge with clinical summary and follow-up plan

### 9. Patient Portal
- Patients log in to view their own appointments, medical records, and profile
- Complete privacy — no patient can see another patient's data
